HashClerk Backend

WIP

As document forgery and integrity become massive issues in both legal and corporate environments, HashClerk provides a cryptographic solution.

The backend is engineered natively in Go to ensure maximum performance and high concurrency when verifying document hashes. It serves as a secure, extremely fast ledger for document verification.
