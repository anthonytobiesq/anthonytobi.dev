Tech Contract AI

In Development

Tech Contract AI bridges the gap between software engineers and legal teams.

When a startup signs a complex SaaS agreement, there are usually layers of hidden liabilities, SLA loopholes, and data privacy concerns. Tech Contract AI takes the raw text of an agreement and uses NLP to flag non-standard clauses specifically related to software deployment, data handling, and European compliance.

Features

  • Automated Redlining: Suggests standard industry language.
  • Data Privacy Checks: Flags clauses violating GDPR standards.
  • Developer Friendly API: Can be integrated into CI/CD pipelines for automated compliance checks before deployment.
