Managing rental properties and tenants in Nigeria presents unique logistical and compliance challenges that generic global software often fails to address. 

**Tenanto** is a specialized tenant management system currently in development, designed from the ground up to handle the specific operational realities, payment structures, and legal nuances of the Nigerian real estate market. 

### Key Features
- **Localized Rent Collection:** Integration with local payment gateways for seamless rent tracking and automated reminders tailored to local lease terms.
- **Lease Agreement Management:** Generate, store, and manage leases that are fully compliant with Nigerian property laws.
- **Maintenance Tracking:** A streamlined communication portal for tenants to log issues and for landlords to coordinate with local service professionals.
- **Financial Reporting:** Clear, easy-to-export ledgers and KPI dashboards tailored for individual landlords and boutique property managers.
