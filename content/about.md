I am Anthony Orobosan Tobi, a Software Developer and Legal Technology professional from Nigeria, currently based in Vilnius, Lithuania.

My journey is unconventional. I spent over a decade as a Legal Officer and Company Secretary in Nigeria, managing board governance and drafting commercial contracts. However, I consistently found myself drawn to the backend systems that underpinned this work. This curiosity led me to pursue an LL.M. in LegalTech at Mykolas Romeris University and dive deep into software engineering.

Today, I build robust systems and automation tools using **Go**, **Python**, **SQL**, and **Linux**. My unique blend of legal expertise and hands-on software development allows me to collaborate effectively with technical teams, automate complex reporting workflows, and ensure that the software I build aligns perfectly with risk and compliance standards (such as GDPR).

### Core Technologies
- **Languages:** Go, Python, SQL
- **Systems & Tools:** Linux, Git, SAP, Salesforce
- **Specialties:** Backend Development, Workflow Automation, LegalTech Integration, Cybersecurity

I am always looking for new engineering challenges where I can leverage my unique analytical background to build impactful software.
